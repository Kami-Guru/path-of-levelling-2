G2_1 Vastiri Outskirts--------------------------------------
Zone layout may be flipped, Boss is either down/left or 
down/right, rougly Z shape

Stay on the tip of Rathbreaker's cliff to avoid spears

Kill Rathbreaker then TP to start and Exit > Town
In Town Caravan > The Halani Gates, don't need to talk to 
Sekhema just get the ! over her head then Exit > Town

In Town Caravan > Mawdun Quarry

G2_10_1 Mawdun Quarry---------------------------------------
Roughly Z Shape. If in doubt follow a wall

Exit > Mawdun Mine

G2_10_2 Mawdun Mine-----------------------------------------
Boss is usually directly north from entrance

RECOMMEND Fire Res, boss can deal a lot of damage. Watch out
for mines

Exit > Traitor's Passage

G2_2 Traitor's Passage--------------------------------------
If you find Decree of Imprisonment lore item, follow pages
on the wall for Balbala, then the other direction for exit

Six Sisters indicate you're going the right way. Could get
really early Six Sisters, there's a layout image for that

Exit > The Halani Gates

G2_3 The Halani Gates---------------------------------------
Head to opposite corner from entrance

RECOMMEND Lightning Resistance, Topaz Charm

In Town Caravan > Keth

G2_4_1 Keth-------------------------------------------------
Wall-follow clockwise.
If you find exit before boss, Checkpoint > entrance and
    wall-follow counter-clockwise
If you find exit before boss AGAIN, Checkpoint > entrance
    and try the other two points in zone layout image
There is a head-shaped tile near boss, looks like a round 
buried building

XP is great here

Exit > The Lost City

G2_4_2 The Lost City----------------------------------------
Zone is shaped like a diamond with bridges as the corners

First bridge is either far left or far right of the first
"rectangle"

[Optional] There is a sarcophagus with Lvl 6/7 Skill

Exit > Buried Shrines

G2_4_3 Buried Shrines---------------------------------------
Zone is two rectangles joined by bridges

The direction the bridge points tells you where the boss is:
South - North: boss is directly up
North - South: boss is directly left

Boss tile has V-shaped bridge facing north-east

[Optional] Elemental Offering event drops a magic 
cold/fire/lightning res ring

Kill the boss, TP > Town then Caravan > Mastodon Badlands

G2_5_1 Mastodon Badlands------------------------------------
Wall-follow the wall opposite the Waypoint

Abyss in this area drops Jawbone (weapon mod)
Effigy in this zone drops Lvl 1 Support

Exit > Lightless Passage

Second time here Exit > The Bone Pits

Abyss_Intro Lightless Passage-------------------------------
Well of Souls is always top-left

Exit > Well of Souls
Then Waypoint > Mastodon Badlands

G2_5_2 The Bone Pits----------------------------------------
Bone Arch near entrance to zone points directly to the boss

Kill boss then TP > Town and Caravan > Valley Of The Titans

G2_6 Valley Of The Titans-----------------------------------
Wall-follow either direction for seals and permanent buff,
third seal might be in the middle of the zone

Exit > The Titan Grotto

G2_7 The Titan Grotto---------------------------------------
Zone is shaped like a rectangle, follow the direction the 
bridge from the entrance takes to get to the opposite side
Boss is on long straight bridge similar to entrance

Usually stay in 12 o'clock for first phase and 3 o'clock for
second phase

Kill boss then TP > Town, use Horn on North tip of Caravan 
Then Caravan > Deshar

G2_8 Deshar-------------------------------------------------
Quest item is on the outside of one of the buildings

Exit is on the furthest edge of second area with buildings

Exit > Path Of Mourning

G2_9_1 Path Of Mourning-------------------------------------
Area is unpredictable, try going to opposite corner of zone
from entrance

Can also try 3-3-3 method of going right for three bridges,
up/right for three bridges, down/right for three bridges,
repeat 

G2_9_2 The Spires Of Deshar---------------------------------
Don't forget the Sisters Of Garukhan!!!
Sisters should be in a G-shaped tile, use process of 
elimination to find it

Kill Tor Gul, TP > Town then Caravan > The Dreadnought

G2_12_1 The Dreadnought-------------------------------------
Be patient, enemies jump behind you, easy to get swarmed

Go roughtly up/left

G2_12_2 Dreadnought Vanguard---------------------------------
Be patient, enemies jump behind you, easy to get swarmed

RECOMMEND lightning resist

Go roughtly up/left
