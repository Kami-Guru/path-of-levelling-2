G3_1 Sandswept Marsh----------------------------------------
In swamp area kill Rootredge for Lvl 9 Skill Gem, usually in
the middle

In grass area Azak Campfire is on north or south of exit,
drops Lesser Jeweller's Orb

Exit > Town then Exit > Jungle Ruins

G3_3 Jungle Ruins-------------------------------------------
Four main locations:
1) Infested Barrens - opposite wall from entrance
2) The Venom Crypts - snakes slither towards entrance
3) Silverfist - usually top third of zone, or in the middle
4) Matlan Waterways

Steps:
1. Start following the downward wall to find 2) or 3)
2. From opposite side checkpoint to the start and wall 
    follow the other way to find 1)
3. If you found all 4 locations then Exit > Infested Barrens
4. Else scan back and forth through the zone starting from
    top/left third of map

[Optional] Jungle grave for a belt
[Optional] Troubled Camp for a vendor, they point to exit

Exit > Venom Crypts

Second time here Exit > Infested Barrens

Third time here Exit > Matlan Waterways

G3_2_1 Infested Barrens-------------------------------------
All locations are on edges of zone so wall-follow to get all
checkpoints and Waypoint

Exit > Chimeral Wetlands

G3_7 The Azak Bog-------------------------------------------
Boss is generally in up-right quarter of the zone

Kill boss, TP > Town then Waypoint > Matlan Waterways

G3_4 The Venom Crypts---------------------------------------
If you spawn on the edge of the zone, corpse is always in
the middle, follow the wall opposite the entrance

If the corpse is not in the middle it can be in any corner,
good luck

Either take the Exit > Jungle Ruins to find Infested Barrens
OR Waypoint > Jungle Ruins if you already found it

G3_5 Chimeral Wetlands--------------------------------------
Wall-follow to the right

Jiquani's Machinarium always faces the left (right wall)
Temple of Chaos always faces down (top wall)

Get waypoint for ToC and Jiquani's Machinarium then 
Exit > Jiquani's Machinarium

G3_6_1 Jiquani's Machinarium--------------------------------
Listen to loot filer sounds if you have one, quest item will
make a sound before you can see it

Blackjaw is in far left or far right of zone

Exit > Jiquani's Sanctum

G3_6_2 Jiquani's Sanctum------------------------------------
For second generator, respawn at checkpoint then walk away a
bit, rinse and repeat until soul core spawns nearby

Checkpoint to start of zone for boss

Kill boss then TP > Town then Waypoint > Infested Barrens to
Exit > The Matlan Waterways

G3_2_2 The Matlan Waterways---------------------------------
RECOMMEND fire resist and patience

Exit > Azak Bog for Spirit + Spirit Gem

Second time here Find the lever, then TP > Town,
then Exit > The Drowned City

G3_8 The Drowned City---------------------------------------
Don't forget the waypoint at the start!

Go up/right until you hit a wall, then down/right until you
find a large area with a checkpoint. This is an ambush
Exit the ambush then turn down/right then up/right for exit

If you see Molten Vault, enter to get the Waypoint, but you
don't need 3-to-1 until endgame so leave this zone for later

Exit > Apex Of Filth

G3_9 The Molten Vault---------------------------------------
Best to leave this zone for after Campaign for fastest clear

Go up/left until you pull lever to cross the lava
Go up/right, cross bridges and enter doors when stuck
Eventually you'll hit a wall, go bottom/right to reach boss

G3_11 Apex Of Filth-----------------------------------------
Zone moves approximately up/right or up/left
Halfway point in the zone is a big arch, after that turn
left, then turn right, and continue to the end of the zone
If the boss isn't there, turn right again and keep checking
that end of the zone

My eyes :(
Kill the boss then TP > Town 
Then go down the big stairs again to Exit > Temple of Kopec

G3_12 Temple of Kopec---------------------------------------
Each floor is a triangle and the exit is in a corner

Door won't show on minimap until you're really close, don't
turn away too early

Second floor has an unpassable hole, the boss room is on the
other side of it. 

G3_14 Utzaal------------------------------------------------
Utzaal has same layout as The Drowned City

Follow the path with the big tiles, boss is in the same 
place that you were ambushed in The Drowned City

Go up/right until you hit a wall, then down/right until you
find boss room
After boss turn down/right then up/right for exit

Exit > Aggorat

G3_16 Aggorat-----------------------------------------------
Find the gigantic area with altar with the enemies praying, 
go through the arch to get to second half of zone

In the second half of zone do the sacrificial heart for 
2 Passive Points

Exit to the Black Chambers is diagonal from the altar area
Exit > The Black Chambers

G3_17 The Black Chambers------------------------------------
Go left or right until you cross first bridge

Continue in the same direction after the bridge to find 
checkpoint with second bridge

Follow the bridges that float out of the water

Kill Doriyani!
