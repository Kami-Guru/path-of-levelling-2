G1_1 The Riverbank------------------------------------------
Kill Hillock and Exit > Town

Thank you for using Path Of Levelling 2!


G1_2 Clearfell----------------------------------------------
[Optional] Mud Burrow boss has Lvl 2 Skill + Lvl 1 Support

Minimap shows a grey-ish path to Mud Burrow if you don't
have transparent terrain

If entering up/left and you immediately see a bridge, Beira
and exit is on your side, Mud Burrow is other side
Otherwise entering up/left means only one bridge, and all 
points of interest are on the other side of the bridge

If entering up/right, anything could be anywhere, good luck

Exit > The Grelwood


G1_3 Mud Burrow---------------------------------------------
Skippable area, Devourer drops Lvl 2 Skill + quest reward
Lvl 1 Support

Boss is directly left/right/up/down from the entrance


G1_4 The Grelwood-------------------------------------------
Four locations, always shaped like a diamond

If you find The Grim Tangle or The Red Vale, go in and grab 
the waypoint before coming back

1) The Grim Tangle - mushrooms spawn near entrance
2) Tree Of Souls - magical faintly glowing roots point to it
3) Brambleghast - Lvl 1 Skill
4) Areagne's Hut - optional potions + Lvl 1 Skill

Exit > The Red Vale, upstream from river on edge of the zone


G1_5 The Red Vale-------------------------------------------
Usually a spiral pattern, try following the zone clockwise
or counterclockwise with the wall just offscreen
Listen for music when altar is nearby
One altar may randomly spawn in the middle of the zone

Refined Arms in this area drops level 5 martial weapon base

Kill the boss, TP > Town, then Waypoint > The Grim Tangle


G1_6 The Grim Tangle----------------------------------------
Go roughly up and left

Zone layout may be flipped vertically, so if you start north
go south

[Optional] Rotten Druid drops a Lvl 1 Support

Exit > Cemetary Of The Eternals


G1_7 Cemetary Of The Eternals-------------------------------
Zone layout may be rotated
Wall-follow around the zone, get both checkpoints first
Exits usually on opposite sides of the zone

Grave on the edge of the zone could have an Iron Ring

Checkpoint > Mausoleum Of The Praetor

Second time here Checkpoint > Tomb Of The Consort


G1_8 Mausoleum Of The Praetor-------------------------------
Draw a line from the exit through the tile in the middle of
the zone, boss is usually in that direction

If you enter at the bottom of the zone, boss could be on the
right of the center

Kill the boss, Exit > Cemetary Of The Eternals
Then Checkpoint > Tomb Of The Consort


G1_9 Tomb Of The Consort------------------------------------
Boss is usually in north corner of the zone or in the middle

Follow the corridors through the center of the zone

TP > Town, Waypoint > Cemetary Of The Eternals for boss


G1_11 Hunting Grounds---------------------------------------
Follow the edge of the zone clockwise

Kill Crowbell for 2 Passive Points. Footprints lead to boss,
also a giant thorny pit nearby

Signpost points to Ogham Farmlands, get Waypoint, come back

Completed Ritual in middle has locusts flying to Freythorn

If you find Ogham Farmlands or Freythorn then go in, get the
waypoint and come back

Exit > Freythorn, or TP Town then Waypoint if you got it

Second time here Exit/TP > Ogham Farmlands


G1_12 Freythorn---------------------------------------------
Completed Rituals have trail of locusts leading you to the
next one

Kill King In The Mist then TP > Town
Then Waypoint > Hunting Grounds to Exit > Ogham Farmlands


G1_13_1 Ogham Farmlands-------------------------------------
Usually go down and left, exit always on opposite wall from
entrance

Last layout image is the Una's Lute tile. Quest item is
always on the other side of the broken down caravans

Exit > Ogham Village


G1_13_2 Ogham Village---------------------------------------
Follow the edge of the zone north

If you find the boss before Renly's Tools take Checkpoint to
the entrance then wall-follow south

Kill The Executioner then Exit > The Manor Ramparts


G1_14 The Manor Ramparts------------------------------------
Layout could be flipped vertically! Usually go clockwise

[Optional] Gallows on north wall has Lvl 1 Support, or south
wall if layout is flipped


G1_15 Ogham Manor-------------------------------------------
Each floor has a bridge roughly in the middle, find that
first

First Floor usually up and right

Second and Third floors go in the opposite direction as 
previous floor

Exit > Act 2!
